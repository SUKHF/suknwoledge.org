Why delay?

Innovating ideas for success

Our pre-incubation program stretches for 12-weeks to support founders at their early startup stages and create sustainable ventures by prioritizing Problem Validation, Market Research, Customer Discovery and thereby understanding nuances of entrepreneurship. The Program provides the opportunity to test, validate and continuously improve on their business ideas and enable entrepreneurs to identify and interact with their target customers to better understand the market-solution fit.

‍

Eligibility Criteria:

Startups with unique and innovative business ideas

Ideas that have commercialization potential

Student innovators & aspiring entrepreneurs at the ideation stage are eligible

‍

Why to apply

Workshops & Experts Insights

Intensive work on Customer Discovery

Pre-Incubation Graduation Certificate

Chance to get incubated at KL Technology Incubators Foundation

High potential ideas may get an opportunity to get Entrepreneur-in-Residence support with a grant up to Rs. 4.00 Lakhs.
